\ques{4}{c}{15} Let $X_1,X_2,\ldots,X_n$ be a random sample from a normal population with mean $\mu$ and variance 25. For a sample size of n=16, find the uniformly most powerful (UMP) test for testing the simple null hypothesis $H_0 : \mu =5$ against the composite alternative hypothesis $(i)H_1: \mu > 5$ and $(ii)H_1: \mu < 5$.
