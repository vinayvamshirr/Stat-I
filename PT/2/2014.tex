\ques{4}{c}{15} Let the probability function of X and Y be 
\[ \begin{aligned}
    P(X=x) &= \binom{n}{x}p^x(1-p)^{n-x}, x = 0,1,\ldots,n; \\
    P(Y=y) &= \binom{y-1}{r -1}p^r(1-p)^{y-r}, y=r,r+1,\ldots
\end{aligned} \] Prove that $P[X \geq r] = P[Y\leq n]$.
