\ques{3}{b}{20} Explain the convergence in $r$th mean and convergence almost sure. Check various modes of convergence for $\{X_n\}$, where $X_n 's$ are independently distributed as follows :
\begin{equation*}
\begin{aligned}
    P[X_n = 0] = 1 - \frac{1}{n^r} \quad &\text{and} \quad P[X_n = n] = \frac{1}{n^r}\\
    \text{where} \quad r\geq 2 \quad &\text{and}  \quad n=1,2,\dots
\end{aligned}
\end{equation*}
