\ques{1}{a}{12} A tyre manufacturing company has three production units \emph{A, B} and $C$ which produce respectively 50\%, 30\% and 20\% of the entire production. Unit A is known to produce 5\% defectives, unit B produces 3\% defectives and unit C produces 1\% defectives. What percentage of items in the entire produce is defective? If an item is found to be defective, what is the probability that it was produced at unit C?
